BUSINESS RULE SPECIFICATIONS

RULE INFORMATION

Statement:  The owner has a firm rule that he never sells a rug at a loss 
unless it has been in his inventory for over two years.

Constraint: No negative profit until current date - purchase date is greater than 2 years. 

Type:  Application Oriented   

Category: Field Specific

Test On: Update               


STRUCTURES AFFECTED

Field Names: Profit, DateAcquired 

Table Names: Rug, Sale


FIELD ELEMENTS AFFECTED

Physical Elements: Data Type

Logical Elements:  Range of Values
